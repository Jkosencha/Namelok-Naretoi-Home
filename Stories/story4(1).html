Grace’s Profile

  • Name: Grace
  • Age: 13
  • Gender: Female
  • Status: Rescued from early marriage

Grace’s story begins in a remote village where tradition once dictated her path. After escaping a forced early marriage, she was brought to Namelok Naretoi Home. With support, counseling, and a renewed sense of hope, Grace began school and discovered a love for learning. She now dreams of becoming a teacher and helping other girls in her community.
