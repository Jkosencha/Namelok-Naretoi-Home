Our History

A journey of passion, purpose, and people.

Namelok’s story began with a simple idea — to make a difference in the lives of children with disabilities. What started as a local initiative has now grown into a strong network of supporters, volunteers, and change-makers. Over the years, we’ve championed inclusive care, reshaped community perceptions, and empowered families through awareness and support.

From the dusty paths of Narok to global partnerships, our mission has remained the same: to ensure every child is seen, supported, and celebrated. Each milestone reminds us why we started — and why we must continue.

As we reflect on our roots and progress, we invite you to be part of this journey — because history is not just about the past, but the steps we take today for a better tomorrow.
