About Namelok Naretoi Home

We are a sanctuary for vulnerable children, committed to healing, educating, protecting, and empowering them through love and support.

Our Programs

Namelok is dedicated to providing a safe space, motivation and love to children from maginalized families.

Access to Healthcare

We provide access to essential medical treatment, physiotherapy, and rehabilitation for children living with disabilities or who have suffered trauma.

Support Inclusive Education

Education is at the heart of transformation. Ensure disabled children and rescued girls receive access to special needs and mainstream education, helping them integrate into schools based on their abilities.

Rescued Girls

Girls rescued from harmful practices such as FGM and child marriage are given safety, counseling, and opportunities to rebuild their lives and dreams.

Provide a Safe and Nurturing Home

We provide a loving, stable environment where children in recovery receive the care and resources they need.

Awareness Program

We run community campaigns that raise awareness on children's rights, disability rights, gender equality, menstrual health and the dangers of harmful cultural practices.
