About Us

Dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children and families through medical support, education, and empowerment - Since 1991.

Namelok Naretoi is a non-profit community based organization started in 1991, that supports disabled children through medical rehabilitation and scholarships. They also rescue girls from FGM and early marriages, providing shelter and scholarships, and offer guidance and counseling programs.
