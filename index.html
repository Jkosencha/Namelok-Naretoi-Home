About Us

Dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children and families through education, support, and empowerment.

Namelok Naretoi is a non-profit organization that supports disabled children through medical rehabilitation and scholarships. They also rescue girls from FGM and early marriages, providing shelter and scholarships, and offer guidance and counseling programs.
